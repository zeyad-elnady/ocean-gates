Ocean Gates is a leading logistics and worldwide shipping provider with a legacy of excellence dating back to 1984. Headquartered in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (KSA), we have grown to become a trusted name in the industry, offering tailored, efficient, and secure supply chain solutions that span the globe.

Air Freight

When time is critical, air freight is the fastest way to get your goods to their destination. We offer standard air freight, express/priority service, air charter services, and comprehensive customs clearance for time-sensitive shipments.

Road Freight

Road freight offers unparalleled flexibility and door-to-door service. We provide Full Truckload (FTL), Less Than Truckload (LTL), specialized transport for temperature-controlled and dangerous goods, plus cross-border international road transport.

Sea Freight

The most cost-effective solution for transporting large volumes over long distances. We offer Full Container Load (FCL), Less than Container Load (LCL), and specialized ocean cargo including temperature-controlled and oversized shipments.

Warehousing & Logistics

State-of-the-art warehousing solutions to optimize your supply chain. We provide inventory management, picking and packing services, distribution, and specialized e-commerce fulfillment to enhance efficiency.

Packaging and Storage

Proper packaging and secure storage are crucial to the safety and integrity of your goods.

Professional Packaging

Our team can assist with professional packaging services to ensure your items are properly secured for transit, whether by air, sea, or road. We use high-quality materials and industry best practices to protect your cargo from damage.
